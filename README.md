## Netflix Content Analysis
**Tools:** PostgreSQL  

**Project Overview:**  
Analyzed Netflix TV shows and movies to understand content trends and catalog composition.  

**Key Tasks:**  
- Designed and populated relational tables for titles and people (directors, cast)  
- Performed queries to count movies, identify oldest and newest releases, and sort titles alphabetically  
- Joined tables to associate titles with directors and cast members  
- Answered analytical questions to identify trends in content type, release year, and genre  

**Key Insights:**  
- Total movies vs TV shows in the catalog  
- Oldest movie and newest TV show added  
- Popular directors and show distribution across genres

## Fortune Company Benefits Analysis
**Tools:** PostgreSQL  

**Project Overview:**  
Evaluated Fortune 1000 companies to understand patterns in employee benefits and organizational performance.  

**Key Tasks:**  
- Created a database of companies with revenue, employee counts, and benefits data  
- Used CASE statements to categorize companies by benefits (Excellent, Good, Basic, Minimal)  
- Applied GROUP BY and HAVING clauses to analyze revenue and benefit tiers  
- Used logical operators and aggregate functions to answer business questions  

**Key Insights:**  
- Identified high-performing companies with excellent benefits  
- Analyzed correlation between revenue and employee benefits  
- Highlighted patterns in PTO and maternity leave across industries
